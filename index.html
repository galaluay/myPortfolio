About Me

Maintenance engineer transitioned to a developer

I graduated from Chulalongkorn University in Mechanical Engineering and started off as a Maintenance Engineer responsible for machine maintenance in hardware aspect, then shifted to software side and system improvement in December 2020.

To streamline the maintenance performance in term of operations, expenditure, management, structure and strategy, I gathered and addressed a variety of issues and needs from the teams, then analyzed the needs and requirements to provide the solutions.

I developed and managed SharePoint sites including other custom solutions with Microsoft Power Platform (PowerApps, Power Automate, Power BI, etc.) with self-taught skills to enhance maintenance performance and respond to customer-based requirements.

Things I've Built

I streamline the existing tools within the Microsoft Power Platform (PowerApps, Power Automate, Power BI, and etc.) to deliver solutions to approach the needs and requirements.

Spare Part Management App

Formerly, the spare part stock was managed via Excel spreadsheets which did not fully serve the needs of the people involved, and it was difficult to trace back the transaction history.

Then, I came up with a PowerApps application which contained all the functions to manage spare part stock and transactions together with Power Automate flows to help monitoring the stock to prevent part shortage. I also created a dashboard to summarize and show the current stock value.

Downtime Record

The downtime record application was built with PowerApps, stored data in SharePoint Lists, and reported the data summary with a Power BI dashboard. It contained functions to record incidents with ticketing system and facilitate knowledge sharing with an easy access to solutions across products to enhance corrective and preventive maintenance efficiency.

Maintenance Helpdesk

Maintenance Helpdesk App is an application to manage requests to the Maintenance Department with ticketing system. It was developed based on the needs of an application to record the team's activities upon requests and track work order status and result. This app also concerns over permission levels which allows users to be able to add or edit only columns they have permissions.

Power BI Reports

  • Built and automated Power BI reports to optimize Weekly Machine Downtime Summary and Analysis.
  • Created and simplified data models from multiple tables to facilitate data calculations and improve performance.
  • Created the calculated columns and measures with the use of DAX Expressions.

Power Automate Flows

  • Created and automated flows to genarate the preventive maintenance and calibration schedules of all equipment and measuring instruments.
  • Created multiple notification flows.
  • Created multiple Canvas Apps in Power Apps synced with Power Automate.
